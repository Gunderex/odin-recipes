Moroccan Chicken

Description

Grilled Moroccan Chicken seasoned with herbs and spices. Best served with rice and Tzatziki sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Olive Oil
  • Cilantro
  • Parsley
  • Cumin
  • Turmeric
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Combine olive oil, herbs, and spices in a food processor and process until smooth.
  2. Place chicken breast and processed marinade in a plastic bag and let marinate for 30 minutes at room temperature.
  3. Remove chicken from marinade and grill for 5-7 minutes per side.
  4. Remove chicken from grill and let rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve over rice with a side of Tzatziki sauce.
