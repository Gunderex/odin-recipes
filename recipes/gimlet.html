Simple Gimlet

Description

A simple drink using gin, lime juice, and simple syrup. Perfect for a hot, Summer day.

Ingredients

  • 2oz of high quality gin
  • 1oz of fresh squeezed lime juice
  • 1oz of simple syrum

Steps

  1. Combine gin, lime juice, and simple syrup in a shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake for at least 30 seconds to combine and dilute.
  3. Serve in chilled glass.
